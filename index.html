Why Circl?

Since completing the Circl Leadership Programme I've become more aware of how important opportunities like these are to aid the social mobility of disadvantaged young people. While searching for career opportunities, I have seen first-hand how difficult it can be to break into corporate industries without the networking and training experiences that such organisations like Circl offer.
Given that Circl aims to both expand the networks of such young people as well as empower them, an internship at Circl would be incredibly fulfilling, something that I know is rare to find in any job. I am open to having my workplace skills challenged to allow myself to grow and as well as helping Circl to reach new heights.
